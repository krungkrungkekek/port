Suzuki Raider Gen 1 2004

The Suzuki Raider R150 (Gen 1) is a 147.3cc, 4-stroke, single-cylinder, DOHC, 4-valve engine underbone motorcycle with a carburetor fuel system, oil-cooled, and a 6-speed transmission.

Suzuki Raider Carb

The Suzuki Raider R150 Carb is a popular underbone motorcycle known for its powerful 150cc carburetor engine, 6-speed transmission, and sporty, commuter-friendly design, often favored for both city rides and customization.

Suzuki Raider 150 FI

The Suzuki Raider R150 FI is a fuel-injected, 150cc motorcycle known for its power, sporty design, and advanced features, positioning it as a top contender in the underbone category.

About!



Owning a motorcycle is more than just transportation—it's a ticket to freedom, savings, and thrill. Picture yourself gliding through traffic while everyone else is stuck in endless lines of cars. Imagine spending far less on gas while enjoying unbeatable mileage. With a motorcycle, every journey is an adventure waiting to happen. It’s compact, convenient, and stylish—a perfect companion for the modern world. And let’s not forget, motorcycles hold a charm and adrenaline that no car can match. Why settle for ordinary when you can ride extraordinary? Upgrade your commute, elevate your weekends, and make every ride memorable. Choose the power, speed, and freedom that only a motorcycle can offer.




Why do you need to buy a motorcycle?


    Efficient Travel: Beat the traffic! Motorcycles can maneuver through tight spaces, reducing your commute time, especially in congested areas.

    Adventure and Thrill: Riding a motorcycle brings a unique sense of freedom and excitement. Every ride feels like an adventure, whether you’re going to work or exploring the open road.

    Cost-Effective: Motorcycles are typically more affordable to purchase, maintain, and fuel compared to cars. Lower insurance costs are also a plus!
